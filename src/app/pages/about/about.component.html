5. Who am I? Just another developer!

I'm a 30 year old software engineer living in Belgium. At the time of writing I'm working as a technical lead / frontend architect at the biggest telco operator in Belgium, Proximus. We are doing some cool stuff with Electron and integrating a web application with a Windows environment using Electron-edge. I've also done some PHP in the past, but focussing totally on JavaScript, Angular, HTML5 and everything frontend from now on!

I'm educated as an Engineer in Computer Science at the University of Ghent but learned my true passion, frontend, totally in my spare time. I'm playing around with websites since the age of 16 and still love it! Besides that I'm trying to understand the business side & the connection with IT a little bit more by attending an Executive master in Enterprise IT Architecture at Antwerp Management School.

I'm busy with some very cool projects on the side. When I find the time I try do some writing about the technologies I use and share those writings in talks to the public at conferences & meetups all over Europe.
